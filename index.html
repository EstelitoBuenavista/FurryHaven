About Us

Learn More About Us

Furry Haven Cafe is a future collaboration of a workplace cafe and a pet sanctuary for pet lovers to bond with adoration, respect, loving and caring for them. A place where the pet lovers love to stay, work and play with their own pets/furry friends.

  • Furry Haven allows their cups to be eco-friendly materials and reusable plastic cups.
  • Furry Haven sells packaging ceramic mugs, glass mugs and pet bowls as souvenirs for the pet lovers.
  • Furry Haven has promotions of a unique packaging design for animal lovers.

What are you waiting for? Visit Furry Haven now!

Why Choose FurryHaven?

FurryHaven aims to serve the best quality leisure time through interaction with different furry buddies. Take a break or study with them. We also provide high quality packaging for different products ranging from cafe to pet essentials.
